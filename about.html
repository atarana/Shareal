About

Shareal is a new platform made to share your belongings with other people, for example if you own something that you don’t use much often, you could just lend it through our platform and get paid for it, it’s a smart way to reduce pollution, garbage and consumerism.

Our platform also helps you to get in touch with people, it’s nothing like other eCommerces because you will talk to people and most likely meet up with them to collect the goods.
